About Our College

Established in 1965, we are a leading government institution offering quality education

Welcome to GCAS

Government College of Advanced Studies is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality higher education to students from all walks of life. Our campus spans over 50 acres with state-of-the-art facilities.

We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce streams, along with professional courses.
